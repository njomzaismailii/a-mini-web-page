Festivals in Kosova

  • Sunny Hill Festival

    Sunny Hill Festival is the biggest festival organized not only in Kosova but also in the region. This three-day musical event is held every August in Prishtina. This festival is founded by the Sunny Hill Foundation which is a charity organization under the great artist Dua Lipa. It helps to reduce injustice and poverty in Kosova. Many famous artists perform at the festival not only local but as well as international. Check out more on the links below:

  • DokuFest

    DokuFest also known as International Documentary and Short Film Festival, held in the beautiful city of Prizren in the season of summer usually in August. The festival is nine-day long and during there are played around 200 short films from all around the world. Worth mentioning are also photo exhibitions, lively music events and debates that are part of the festival.   • Hardh Fest

    Hardh Fest is a festival that takes place in Rahovec. The four-day festival contains concerts, promoting the local wine and raki. Based on the fact that city of Rahovec is known for its gigantic grape harvest. With the amazing atmosphere of getting to experience local traditions and music.   • PriFest

    Prishtina International Film Festival (PriFest) is the biggest film event in Kosova. This festival has four competition programs: the Balkan film competition program, the European film competition, the Middle-length competition and Documentary film competition. It also has non-competitive programs named the Focus program, which supports and promotes the cinematography of a specific country. PriFest is a five-day festival usually held in summer season, specifically in July, in Prishtina. Find out more about PriFest from the link below:
