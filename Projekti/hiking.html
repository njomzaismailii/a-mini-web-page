Hiking and other fun activites in the nature of Kosova

  • Hiking in the mountains of Kosova

    The best part about visiting Kosova are the beautiful mountains that sorround the country. Mountains that contain perfect views of Kosova, like hidden gems of the beautiful nature. What's more exciting about these mountains is the fact that they are easy to climb and available every season of the year. Fortunately hiking in Kosova is highly developed and every weekend there are events organised to visit mountains around Kosova.   • Climb on the rock to reach the caves

    A must fun to-do activity is Via Ferrata (from italian metal route). Via Ferrata offers you climbing to seven different caves, including Queen's cave and King's cave near city of Peja. With a professional guide and a certain security you have three offers you can choose:

    1. Via Ferrata “Ari” (Length 450m/ climbing 90 mins/ Walking 30-40 mins = Total 2.30-3.00 hours)
    2. Via Ferrata “MAT” (Length 500m/ climbing 90 mins/ Walking 10 mins = Total 1.40 hours)
    3. Via Ferrata “MARIMANGAT” (Length 1 km/ climbing 4.30 hours / walking 30 mins = Total 5 hours)
    If you want to go this weekend check the links below and find out more:
    • Via Ferrata Peja
    • Marimangat

  • Skiing in the snowy Kosova

    While visiting Kosovo during winter season, December-February, you can visit the amazing Brezovica known the best place where you and your loved ones can go ski. Besides Brezovica there are other places also such as Boge and Prevalla. Skiing and enjoying the view Kosova can bring during winter is the best time to also spend the holidays.   • Kayaking in splendid waters of Kosova

    Another fun activity that you can have in Kosovo is kayaking in the waters of Badoc lake and Drini i Bardhe river. This fun activity is organised by Super Campers. Super Campers is an organization the has plans many events on the beautiful albanian nature. Kayaking is an activity that takes place mainly in summer when the weather is warm and the sky is blue but not just that. Furthermore check out about this event in the links below:
